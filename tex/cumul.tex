\maketitle
I plotted cumulative distirbution functions (CDFs) from the perviously calculated CSV histograms \ref{sec:CDF} and used the CDFs to sample the histograms \ref{sec:sampled}.
I also did some statistical tests (Kolmogorov-Smirnov and $\chi^2$-test, as previously) between the ,,measured'' and sampled ones \ref{sec:tests}.
All histograms passed the tests although the p-values for b- and c-jet distributions at $p_t>160$ GeV and $\eta\in[1.6,\,2.5)$ were relatively small.
Relevant source files are src/cumulative.cpp and src/genrand.cpp.
